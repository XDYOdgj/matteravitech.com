Melorca&Guilla 4PCS Faux Nandina Branches

These artificial Nandina branches are made from high-quality, durable plastic and feature a realistic design that closely mimics the look and texture of real plants. They provide a fresh, green aesthetic without the need for watering, pruning, or sunlight, making them perfect for adding a touch of nature and elegance to any space. These branches are ideal for both indoor and outdoor use, whether adorning your home, office, or garden.

Each Nandina branch is approximately 43.3 inches long and includes one primary branch along with three smaller secondary branches. Designed for use in vases, they are easy to customize and fit into various vase sizes and decor styles. These fake greenery stems are great for creating a lush arrangement for a floor vase or a simpler, elegant design for a desktop. Perfect for weddings, birthdays, or festive decorations, they bring a sense of tranquility and good luck to any occasion.
